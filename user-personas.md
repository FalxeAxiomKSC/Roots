# User Personas: Sustainability Funding Surfacing Interface

## Persona 1: Maya Chen - Community Food Cooperative Director

### 📋 Overview
- **Name:** Maya Chen
- **Role:** Executive Director, Green Valley Community Food Cooperative
- **Age:** 42
- **Location:** Rural Vermont, USA
- **Cooperative Type:** Food distribution and retail
- **Experience:** 8 years in cooperative management
- **Tech Comfort:** Intermediate - uses basic software but prefers simplicity

### 🎯 Goals & Motivations
- **Primary Goal:** Secure funding to expand the cooperative's urban farming program
- **Secondary Goals:** 
  - Increase membership by 30% within 2 years
  - Establish a year-round farmers market
  - Provide job training for local youth
- **Motivations:** Community food security, supporting local farmers, creating sustainable jobs

### 💡 Pain Points
- **Funding Research:** "I spend 20+ hours/week searching for grants that might fit our mission"
- **Complex Applications:** "The paperwork for foundation grants is overwhelming for our small staff"
- **Timeline Management:** "We miss opportunities because application deadlines sneak up on us"
- **Finding the Right Fit:** "Many funders don't understand the cooperative business model"

### 🛠️ Technical Profile
- **Device Usage:** Primarily laptop, occasional tablet use in the field
- **Internet Access:** Reliable broadband at office, spotty at farm sites
- **Preferred Features:** Simple search, clear deadlines, mobile-friendly forms
- **Frustrations:** Slow websites, complicated navigation, mandatory account creation

### 📊 Key Information Needs
- **Funding Types:** Agricultural grants, community development funds, food security programs
- **Budget Range:** $50,000 - $500,000
- **Geographic Focus:** Vermont and New England region
- **Timeline:** 6-18 month projects
- **Reporting Requirements:** Monthly progress reports, financial statements, impact metrics

### 🎨 Design Preferences
- **Visual Style:** Clean, professional but approachable
- **Information Density:** Moderate - not too sparse, not overwhelming
- **Color Preferences:** Earth tones, greens, natural imagery
- **Typography:** Clear, readable fonts with good contrast

### 📱 Usage Scenarios
1. **Morning Routine:** Check application deadlines and new opportunities while drinking coffee
2. **Field Work:** Research funding opportunities using tablet at farmers market
3. **Team Meeting:** Present funding options to the cooperative board
4. **Evening Follow-up:** Submit applications and track progress after work hours

## Design Implications

### Common Needs Across Personas
1. **Simplified Search:** Easy to find relevant funding opportunities
2. **Clear Deadlines:** Prominent display of application due dates
3. **Progress Tracking:** Simple status updates for applications
4. **Mobile Access:** All personas need mobile-friendly interfaces
5. **Accessibility:** Support for different tech comfort levels

### Unique Requirements
- **Maya:** Community-focused design, local emphasis
- **Jamal:** Data-rich interfaces, technical sophistication
- **Elena:** Simple design, multilingual support
- **David:** Offline capabilities, practical functionality

### Success Criteria
- All personas can find relevant funding opportunities in under 5 minutes
- Application completion rates improve by 40%
- User satisfaction scores above 4.5/5
- Mobile usage accounts for 60%+ of interactions
